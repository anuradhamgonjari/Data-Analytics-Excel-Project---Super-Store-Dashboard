1)📌 Project Overview:

The Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard is an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, product categories, and regional trends. The dashboard enables users to explore business performance using dynamic slicers and Pivot Charts, providing valuable insights for data-driven decision-making.

2)🎯 Objectives:

Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
Monitor monthly sales trends.
Compare sales across regions, customer segments, and product categories.
Evaluate shipping mode performance.
Identify high-performing product sub-categories.
Enable interactive filtering using slicers.

7)📌 Key Business Insights:

West region generated the highest sales revenue.
Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
Technology category achieved the highest sales.
Phones and Chairs were among the top-performing product sub-categories.
Standard Class accounted for the majority of shipments.
Monthly sales showed an upward trend toward the end of the year.
